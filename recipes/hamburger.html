Hamburger

This is how you make a hamburger though honestly anyone should be able to slap beef on a grill and put it between two bread buns.

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Two buns
  • Some pickles
  1. Put the ground beef on a skillet and let it cook until brown
  2. While the beef is browning, toast the buns
  3. Once the beef is done, put it between the two buns
  4. add your onions on top
  5. add your pickles, enjoy!
