hotdog

Here's another recipe so I can say I went through this, at least, with a little effort.

  • One Hotdog
  • One bun
  • Some Chili
  • Some Cheese
  1. Put your hotdog on the grill until it looks tasty
  2. While cooking, put the cheese at the bottom of the bun
  3. Put the hot dog on the cheese once it's done cooking to melt the cheese
  4. Put the chili on top
